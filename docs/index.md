# Wine Cellar

Track your wine from crush to bottle — and catch problems before they ruin a batch.

## What is Wine Cellar?

Wine Cellar is a mobile-first web app that keeps tabs on your wine batches so you don't have to rely on sticky notes and memory. It follows each batch from crushing through bottling, watches your fermentation around the clock, and warns you when something needs attention.

## What you get

- **Never lose track of a batch** — Follow every batch through each stage of winemaking
- **Watch fermentation in real time** — See gravity and temperature from manual entries or a RAPT Pill hydrometer
- **Keep a complete record of what you did** — Log additions, measurements, rackings, tastings, and notes
- **Get warned before problems cost you a batch** — Stall detection, temperature alerts, missing readings, and stage suggestions
- **Do the math without a calculator** — ABV, chaptalization, sulfite dosing, hydrometer correction, and calibration tools
- **Compare this year's fermentation against last year's** — Overlay curves from up to five batches
- **Take your data with you** — Download readings and activities as CSV
- **Stay in the loop when you're away from the screen** — Push notifications on your phone or desktop
- **Use it like a native app** — Install on your home screen for quick access
